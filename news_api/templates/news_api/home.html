Project Overview

The News App allows you to stay up to date with the latest headlines across various categories. Built using Django and integrated with a third-party news API, users can easily explore news by category or country. The app features an intuitive search functionality, enabling users to find articles based on their preferences and interests.

The key functionalities include:

  • View the latest news by category (Health, Business, Entertainment, etc.)
  • Search for articles based on country and keywords
  • Read articles in detail by redirecting to the source
  • Comment on news articles and interact with other users
